Biography

Sin Bandera ("Flagless" or "Without A Flag") a R&B, Latin pop, Romantic duo spontaneously formed on 2000 in Mexico City. Throughout the duo's trajectory they became successfully recognized within the latin america community. They carried out their tour in Venezuela, Boliva, Argentina, Paraguay, Chile, Ecuador, Colombia, Mexico, the United States, Spain and among other countries.

The duo won various awards including the Latin Grammy, ¡Oye!, Lo Nuestro, MTV Latino and many more. "Entra En Mi Vida"("Come Into My Life"), their first single on 2002, It's main theme of the Mexican telenovela Cuando Seas Mía ("When You're Mine"). "Amor Real" ("Real Love"), which also served as the main theme for that homonymous soap opera in 2003. On 2008 the duo split to accomplish their own projects. However on 2015 they announced their reunion and "Una Ultima Vez" (On last time").
